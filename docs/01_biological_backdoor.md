## 1. The "Chlormequat" Receipt (The Infertility Agent)

**Compound:** Chlormequat Chloride
**Function:** Plant Growth Regulator (stunts height to prevent bending).
**The Loophole:** In 2018, the EPA raised the tolerance for residue on imported oats. In 2020, they raised it again.

### The Data (Temkin et al., 2024)

- **Source:** _Journal of Exposure Science & Environmental Epidemiology_ [DOI: 10.1038/s41370-024-00643-4]
- **Finding:** Chlormequat found in **80%** of human urine samples (2017-2023).
- **The Spike:**
  - 2017 Detectability: **69%**
  - 2023 Detectability: **90%**
- **The Carriers:**
  - Quaker Oats (Old Fashioned): **Detected**
  - Cheerios (General Mills): **Detected**
  - **Organic Control Group:** None detected.
- **Biological Impact:** Documented disruption of steroidogenesis (hormone production) and reduction in sperm motility in mammalian models (swine/mice).

## 2. The "Paraquat" Receipt (The Neurotoxin)

**Compound:** Paraquat Dichloride
**Function:** Herbicide (Desiccant).
**The Discrepancy:** Banned in the EU (2007), China (2020), and Brazil. **Legal in the US.**

### The Parkinson's Link

- **Mechanism:** Paraquat creates oxidative stress that kills dopamine-producing neurons.
- **The Receipt:** NIH study (Agricultural Health Study) showed a **2.5x increased risk** of Parkinson's Disease in applicators.
- **Status:** Syngenta (manufacturer) internal documents released in litigation ("The Paraquat Papers") suggest knowledge of neurotoxicity since the 1970s.
